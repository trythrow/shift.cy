The Thesis

The way software gets built is changing. Fast.

LLMs and agentic coding are reshaping the SDLC in real time. The engineers and product leaders who master the new tools and workflows early will have a serious advantage. ShiftCy is where those people find each other.

Why ShiftCy exists

Code is increasingly cheap to produce. An engineer with the right agentic setup can output in hours what used to take days. The competitive edge is shifting from writing code to mastering tools, designing automated workflows, and managing cost. Most companies in Cyprus are still catching up. This group is where people work through it together, at a high level.

The opportunity

The titles on LinkedIn may look the same, but what's expected of a senior engineer or product lead is changing fast. The people who build hands-on experience with agentic tools and workflows now will be the ones companies turn to when they're ready to move.

The edge

The people who invest time now in understanding agentic workflows will have a real head start. This is the window to become the person your company (or your next one) calls when they need to actually ship with AI, not just pilot it.

What we believe

The ideas driving this community

01

Code is becoming a commodity

LLMs write code. Fast. The value is shifting from producing code to orchestrating how it gets produced, reviewed, tested, and deployed.

02

Tooling mastery is the new multiplier

The gap between engineers is increasingly about tooling. Those who master agentic workflows can ship dramatically faster at a fraction of the cost.

03

Roles are shifting under the surface

Job titles may not have changed yet, but the responsibilities and expectations already have. The skills that made a strong resume two years ago won't be enough. What's being asked of engineers and product people is quietly being rewritten.

04

Workflow beats hustle

Smart automated workflows that compound over time beat brute-force engineering every time. Design the system, not just the feature.

05

Cost is a feature

Knowing how to optimize AI spend (model selection, caching strategies, context planning) is as important as knowing how to build.

06

The window is open

The people building real experience with agentic workflows now will have a meaningful advantage as adoption accelerates. That window won't stay open.
